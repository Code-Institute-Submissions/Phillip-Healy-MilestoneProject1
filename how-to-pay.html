How Best to Support

Hammock Software Games is still in its infancy, the absolute best way to support us at the moment is to
download our game, leave a review, and talk to your friends about it! If you feel like supporting even further;
below are a few ways!

Patreon

Patreon is an American membership platform that provides business tools for content creators to run a subscription service. It helps creators and artists earn a monthly income by providing rewards and perks to their subscribers. Patreon is currently the main way my work is subsidised, for the price of a cup of coffee once a month you can help to support game development! To find my patreon Click Here!

Collaboration

If you would like to work together I'm always open to new projects. I mainly code in Python and GDScript but I'm eager to learn new languages and will take up any challenge! To reach out simply drop over to my contact form and I'll be back to you within 24 hours! Click Here!
