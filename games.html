The Games!

To date Hammock Software Games have released one game, there are
a couple more in the works and we will showcase all here.

Viral Invasion

The first game I made that I felt was worth putting out into the world. Built in Godot Engine and completely free to play. This is a skill based arcade game where the concept is simple; tap to jump in the direction you're pointing. Hit the next cell to continue, the game gets harder the further you go!

Picnic Panic

Viral Invasion turned out to be such a hit with my kids and other parents and their kids that I wanted to focus on a kid friendly game for my next project. Picnic Panic is a calm tower defence style game where you protect your picnic from ants!

Busker Blues

I've been a lover of rythm games since I first accidentally rented Bust-A-Groove instead of Boost A Move! I imagined a busker based rythm game with a great splash of role play learning the stories of the people who pass by as you upgrade your song list, instruments and stake better claims as you progress!
