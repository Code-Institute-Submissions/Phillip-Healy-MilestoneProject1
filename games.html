Viral Invasion

My name is Phillip healy, I'm a 34 year old Irishman living in Kerry. I have 2 young kids who are a constant source of learning and inspiration for me. Gaming has been a lifelong passion that I've had for as long as I can remember, from Mario and Zelda, to Donkey Kong, to FInal Fantasy, World of Warcraft, all the way to more contemporary titles such as Hades and Yakuza.

What Do I Do?

I love coding, and have always wanted to make my own games. Doing so while maintaining a work/life balance, putting myself through university and raising a family has been incredibly difficult, but when Covid hit and shut everything down I tried to turn that into an opportunity. I set aside some of my work from home time and began diving into game engines. At first I tried Pygame and built a few basic games like Pong, and Space Invaders. I then moved on to Godot and was delighted that GDScript was derived from Python and a lot of my learning could be carried over.

What Inspires Me?

Having played a huge amount of videogames I knew I'd love to make my own games, but had no idea what kind, or how. It wasn't until I found another hobby; boardgaming that my passion for game design took off. Marrying immersive themes with engaging mechanics is something that can make or break a game and boardgames show this off fantastically. On top of this I started running my own Dungeons and Dragons group which allowed me to put into writing many of the worlds and characters I had bouncing around my head.

What Do I Make?

When I started coding my own games from scratch I knew I wanted games that were family friendly, I have my own young family and I would love to have games they could play. Something intuitive that wouldn't require any reading, limited actions but challenging as you progress to keep the attention of adults as well as children.
